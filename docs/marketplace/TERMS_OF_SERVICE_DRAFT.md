# Terms of Service Draft

Draft status: marketplace preparation draft, not legal advice. This document should be reviewed by counsel before publication or App Store submission.

## Overview

ShopOps Studio is a Shopify embedded app that provides operational reporting for merchants, including location performance, product sales, inventory insights, staff attribution, fixed expenses, and sync/data-quality visibility.

By installing or using ShopOps Studio, the merchant agrees to use the app only for lawful business purposes and in accordance with Shopify's platform rules and applicable laws.

## Use of the Service

Merchants may use ShopOps Studio to:

- Sync Shopify store data into reporting views.
- Review store, location, product, staff, inventory, and financial metrics.
- Configure fixed expenses for reporting.
- Manage app-level access by staff/location where supported.
- Monitor sync health and data quality.

Merchants may not:

- Use the app to violate Shopify policies or applicable laws.
- Attempt to access data belonging to another shop.
- Reverse engineer, overload, interfere with, or abuse the app or its infrastructure.
- Share app access with unauthorized users.
- Use reports as the sole source of truth for regulated accounting, tax, legal, or payroll decisions.

## Reporting and Financial Disclaimer

ShopOps Studio provides operational reporting and analytics only. It does not provide accounting, legal, tax, financial, payroll, or professional advice.

Merchants remain responsible for validating all financial reports, including sales, discounts, refunds, returns, taxes, shipping, cost of goods sold, gross profit, expenses, and net profit. Reports may depend on synced Shopify data, merchant-configured expenses, available inventory costs, Shopify API behavior, and sync timing.
